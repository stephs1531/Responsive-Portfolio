About Me

I am an interactive designer from Atlanta, GA. At my high school art show, an anonymous judge said I might make a good designer someday, so I have spent my life since then working towards perfecting my design skills.

I am a kick-ass horseback rider and was nationally ranked when I was actively competing. I also love ashtanga and acro-yoga and do scratchboard art whenever I get a chance.

The first concert I ever went to was The Cheetah Girls. I was older than I care to admit. I am an easy-mac connoisseur and a total snob when it comes to brands of microwavable macaroni and cheese.
